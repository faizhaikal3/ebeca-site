eBECATerengganu.com

"Tradition Meets Innovation"

eBECATerengganu.com is a digital initiative to promote electric beca (trishaws) as a modern, eco-friendly, and culturally rich transport option in Kuala Terengganu.

About the eBECA Project

The electric beca project in Kuala Terengganu aims to rejuvenate the traditional trishaw experience using sustainable technology.

  • Eco-Friendly: Zero emissions
  • Comfortable: Smooth and silent rides
  • Supported By: Kuala Terengganu Municipal Council and Haji Nasar
